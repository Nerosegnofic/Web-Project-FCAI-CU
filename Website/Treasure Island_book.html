Treasure Island book


Author : Robert Louis Stevenson

Description : "Treasure Island" is an adventure novel by
               Robert Louis Stevenson that tells the story of young Jim
               Hawkins and his experiences with pirates and buried treasure.
               Jim comes into possession of a map leading to the treasure of
               the infamous pirate Captain Flint, and sets out on a dangerous
               journey to claim it. Along the way, he encounters a host of 
               colorful characters, including the cunning and ruthless Long 
               John Silver, and learns about loyalty, courage, and the dangers
               of greed. The novel is known for its atmospheric setting, memorable
               characters, and thrilling action,and has had a significant influence
               on depictions of pirates in popular culture.
