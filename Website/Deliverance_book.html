Deliverance book


Author : James Dickey

Description : Deliverance is a novel written by James Dickey,
             published in 1970, that tells the story of four middle-aged men who
             embark on a canoe trip in the Georgia wilderness. The novel explores
             themes of masculinity, survival, and the human condition. The men
             encounter dangerous mountain men, leading to violent confrontations
             and a struggle for survival. The novel is known for its vivid 
             descriptions of the wilderness and the psychological impact of the
             events on the characters.t was adapted into a successful film in 1972,
             directed by John Boorman.
