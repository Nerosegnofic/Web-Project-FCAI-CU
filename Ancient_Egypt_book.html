Ancient Egypt Book


Author : Lucia gahlin 

Description : Ancient Egypt by Lucia Gahlin is a comprehensive
              guide to the mythology, religion, and history of ancient Egypt.
              The book explores the awe-inspiring temples, tombs, and other 
              sacred sites of the world's first great civilization. It includes
              a chronology of ancient Egypt that charts the evolution of the 
              society that emerged over 5,000 years ago and flourished for three
              millennia. The book is written in an engaging and accessible style,
              making it an ideal introduction to the wonders of ancient Egypt.
